About

I'm a human-centered researcher, designer, and strategist working at the intersections of healthcare, social work, policy, and technology. I strive to drive real change, make good trouble, put people first and stay inquisitive in everything I do. I started my career in criminal justice reentry and homeless services.

Over the years, I gravitated towards big data, design, and user experience methods to improve service delivery, deepen stakeholder engagement, and achieve better outcomes. I hold Masters' in Social Work and Urban Planning degrees from the University of Michigan and a UX certificate from UCLA Extension.
